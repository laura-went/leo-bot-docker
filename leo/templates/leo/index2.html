Talk to Leo.

Leo bot is part of a research project at Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. Leo tries to help you with your emotions by leading a conversation with different topics. Also basic casual chat is possible, but Leo focuses on helping you with several difficult topics in life. Try it out!

Start here

Emotions

Leo analyzes several emotions, such as sadness, happiness, anger, fear and more. Leo uses this emotion detection for determining the next questions.

Avatar

The avatar talks and moves as a response to what you are saying. This makes him feel more trustworthy.

Techniques

Leo makes use of Artificial Intelligence to understand the response and emotion. For example, through natural language processing it determines what you are saying.
